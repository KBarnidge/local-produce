What Is Local Produce?

Local Produce is a web-app designed to provide users with a "One-Stop-Shop"

We are here to help connect the bridge between local farmers and neighborhoods. Whether you are a professional micro-grower, or just a backyard farming enthusiast that wants to share the excess fruits of your labor.

We here at Local Produce hope that through giving farmers a platform to promote themselves, and a marketplace for users to find locally-sourced goods that this will promote a sense of community, bolster the local economy, combat food deserts and reduce the carbon footprint of one's purchases.
